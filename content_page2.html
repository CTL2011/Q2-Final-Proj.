Programs in the Philippines that Support No Hunger

  1. Walang Gutom 2027 Program

    2. -The Walang Gutom Program is a national Program led by the Philippine Governent to reduce hunger and malnutrition among its unfortunate residents. People included in the program receive 3000 pesos monthly through a card which they can use to buy food in stores that accept it as payment. Aside from that, the program also provides education in relation to nutrition to help families become more self-sufficient. The program itself has expanded to 22 provinces, and has reaches 300,000 households, farm from the goal to reach 1 million families by 2026. This program was also funded by a number of government agencies in a collaborative effort to reduce people affected by malnutrition.

  2. Hunger Releif in the Philippines

    3. Action Against Hunger supports disaster-affected communities with cash, livelihood support, and improved access to clean water. The organization also works to improve primary healthcare and nutrition for mothers and children. The program also has a disaster risk reduction initiative so that the risks of damage in vulnerable areas are reduced. In all, the program improves food security, reinforces local health systems, and builds long-term resilience in vulnerable communities.

  3. Hunger Free Philippines

    4. Hunger Free Philippines is a nonprofit organization that aims to feed the hungry and change lives. They provide free services like delivering food,groceries and other essentials to families in extreme poverty, especially in communities where it is rare for help to be received.
